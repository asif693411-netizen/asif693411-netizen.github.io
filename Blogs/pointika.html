Pointika Story

Digital Media & Storytelling Sub-Brand of Point Media

How Pointika Started

Pointika was created as the creative storytelling arm of Point Media. Its mission is to transform ideas into impactful digital stories that connect brands, influencers, and audiences across platforms.

The Vision

Founded by Syed Asif Ali, Pointika focuses on ethical storytelling, content authenticity, and sustainable digital growth rather than fake hype.

What Makes Pointika Different

  • Story-first digital campaigns
  • Influencer collaboration with credibility
  • Organic brand visibility (no fake engagement)
  • Long-term digital reputation building
  • MENA-focused & global reach

Connection With Point Media

Pointika operates as a sub-brand under Point Media, handling creative storytelling, influencer content, and social campaigns, while Point Media manages strategy, consulting, and digital reputation.
