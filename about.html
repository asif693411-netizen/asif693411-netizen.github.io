About Syed Asif Ali

Syed Asif Ali is a young entrepreneur and digital creator from South Waziristan, UAE-based, focusing on social awareness and digital media projects. Born on 23 January 1997, he is the owner of Point Media and known for creating viral content across TikTok and Instagram.

His vision is to grow his digital media brand and contribute positively to the digital content community.
