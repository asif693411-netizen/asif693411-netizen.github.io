My Story

I was born on 23 January 1997 in South Waziristan Agency, Pakistan, into a humble family. Despite facing educational and regional challenges due to conflict, I pursued learning and digital opportunities. Over the years, I moved to UAE and Pakistan, building a career in digital media, content creation, and strategic branding. Today, I lead Point Media and Pointika, helping brands and individuals establish their digital presence.
