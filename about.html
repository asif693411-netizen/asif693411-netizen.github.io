Biography

Born in South Waziristan, Pakistan, and currently based in the UAE, Syed Asif Ali is a visionary entrepreneur who has spent years perfecting the art of digital influence. He is the founder of Point Media, a premier communications brand, and Pointika, a creative sub-brand dedicated to the future of storytelling.

Through his platforms, Syed bridges the gap between raw creativity and strategic brand growth, helping entities achieve global visibility through ethical and authoritative media practices.

Ecosystem Overview

The Parent Brand: Point Media

The core agency focused on strategic communications, reputation management, and building high-authority digital entities for public figures and global businesses.

The Creative Arm: Pointika

A specialized sub-brand under Point Media that handles creative storytelling, influencer collaborations, and engaging social media content ecosystems.
